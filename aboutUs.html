About Us

Brother Nature is a blog started by two people, Arvin Guia and Lemuel Querubin.

Our aim is to create a blog that raises issues, and awareness about our home, the Earth. We post tips, guides, news, and anything related to the Environment.

We need to be knowledgable and informed about the stakes at hand if we do not do anything to save the Environment.

Staying up to date with all the events can inspire us to make a change and be the change. Living in a waste-free world is truly beneficial for each and every living organism in our planet.

Working together by volunteering or donating to causes can be of help to Mother Nature.

Even doing the bare minimum, not littering, recycling properly, or taking care of the trees and plants in our community.

Be a bro, and help us achieve our goal.

Let's do our part, even if it's just one small step at a time.

It’s easy being green - Reduce, Reuse, Recycle.
